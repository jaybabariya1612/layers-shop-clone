Warranty

Your Anarc Smartwatch is backed by a 1-year limited warranty that kicks in from the moment you unbox it. This coverage lasts for 12 months from your purchase date, regardless of usage. Note that Mobile/Laptop skins are not covered under this warranty.
If you encounter any issues, simply get in touch with us, and we’ll assist you with troubleshooting or getting your watch issues fixed/replaced under the warranty.
To save your hassle, we don't have physical service centers. The good part is that we offer the convenience of claiming the warranty at your doorstep. We will offer remote assistance for software issues, and any hardware issues will be addressed through doorstep pickup and delivery.

What’s Covered:

We’ve got you covered for certain defects during the warranty period for Anarc which includes:
-Battery Woes: If your battery isn’t performing like it should, we’ll take care of it. Just keep in mind that battery life may diminish over time.
-Software Snags: Any hiccups with the factory-installed software or firmware are on us. Most software issues can be resolved remotely, so you’re in good hands!

What’s Not Covered:

We want you to get the most out of your smartwatch experience, but there are a few things that aren’t covered by our warranty, like:

- Physical Damage: Scratches, dings, or any damage from mishaps.

- Normal Wear and Tear: Because life happens, and your watch might show signs of love.

- Unauthorized Fixes: If you’ve let someone else tinker with your watch, we can’t cover that.


Remember, this warranty won’t cover:

- Damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs.

- Issues from exposure to liquids, extreme temperatures, or pesky critters.

- Regular wear and tear from everyday use.

Snapmint Payment Plan Terms

We’ve partnered with Snapmint to offer flexible payment options, making it easier for you to own the products you love. Here’s a quick overview of how the Snapmint payment plan works:

- Snapmint’s payment plan is available to eligible customers at checkout based on Snapmint's criteria.

- Eligibility may depend on your credit profile and prior payment history with Snapmint.

- You may be required to make a small down payment at the time of order placement to activate the Snapmint payment plan.

- The remaining balance is split into EMIs that can be paid over 3/6 months.
